Pronunciation[edit]    Rhymes: -ækt    Verb[edit] counteract (third-person singular simple present counteracts, present participle counteracting, simple past and past participle counteracted)  To act in opposition to; thwart; to hinder, defeat, or frustrate, by contrary agency or influence; as, to counteract the effect of medicines; to counteract good advice.

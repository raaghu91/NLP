Trite      Trite planiceps   Scientific classification   Kingdom: Animalia   Phylum: Arthropoda   Class: Arachnida   Order: Araneae   Family: Salticidae   Subfamily: Euophryinae   Genus: Trite Simon, 1885   Type species   Trite pennata Simon, 1885   Species    see text    Diversity   18 species    Trite is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders). Most of the 18 described species occur in Australia and New Zealand, with several spread over islands of Oceania, one species even reaching Rapa.

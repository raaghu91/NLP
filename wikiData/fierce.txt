Fierce   Origin London, United Kingdom   Genres R&B, soul, pop   Instruments Vocals   Years active 1998–2000   Labels Wildstar Records (1998–2000)   Website Fierce.co.uk (defunct)      Members Chantal Alleyne Aisha Peters Sabrina Weathers   Fierce was a British three-piece, all girl R&B group. They were signed to Colin Lester's and Ian McAndrew's Wildstar Records, and scored four hit singles on the UK Singles Chart in 1999 and 2000.[1] The biggest of the hits, "Sweet Love 2K", was a cover of the Anita Baker song "Sweet Love".

See also stupid. Pronunciation[edit]  Rhymes: -ɛndəs  Adjective[edit] stupendous (comparative more stupendous, superlative most stupendous)  Astonishingly great or large; huge; enormous.  One cannot appreciate how stupendous the Matterhorn is without seeing it.   Of stunning volume, degree, or excellence; marvelous.  The renovators created a stupendous new look for our house.

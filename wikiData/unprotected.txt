Lasky Feature Play Company    Distributed by Paramount Pictures    Release dates     November 6, 1916 (1916-11-06)       Running time  50 minutes   Country United States   Language English   Unprotected is a 1916 American drama silent film directed by James Young and written by James Hatton. The film stars Blanche Sweet, Theodore Roberts, Ernest Joy, Tom Forman, Walter Long and Mrs. Lewis McCord. The film was released on November 6, 1916, by Paramount Pictures.[1][2] Cast[edit]  Blanche Sweet as Barbara King Theodore Roberts as Rufus Jamison Ernest Joy as Gov.

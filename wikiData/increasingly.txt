  Increasingly      EP by 12012   Released April 28, 2004   Genre Hard rock   Length 17:40   Language Japanese   Label Under Code Productions   12012 chronology       Increasingly (2004) Bell Salem (2004)      Increasingly is the first EP by the band 12012, released on April 28, 2004. Track listing[edit]  "Gallows" - 3:14 "Incident" - 3:58 "Humanities" - 00:40 "Chouchou -Tefutefu-" (蝶々～てふてふ～) - 4:39 "Itsumo Kokoro ni Juujika wo" (いつも心に十字架を) - 5:08  Notes[edit]  Increasingly would later be remastered and be released as "Increasingly -Kanzen Ban-". Only 3000 copies of the album were pressed. The name was incorrectly printed as "Increasingry" on the album.

Pronunciation[edit]  (US) IPA(key): /sɛˈseɪʃən/ Rhymes: -eɪʃən  Noun[edit] cessation (plural cessations)  (formal) A ceasing or discontinuance, as of action, whether temporary or final.  Motley  the temporary cessation of the papal iniquities   Sir J. Hayward  The day was yearly observed for a festival by cessation from labour.

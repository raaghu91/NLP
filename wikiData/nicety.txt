Released: 1991       Professional ratings   Review scores   Source Rating   Allmusic [1]   Michel'le is the debut album by singer, Michel'le. The album was released in 1989 for Ruthless Records and was produced by Dr. Dre and DJ Yella (both of N.W.A), and executive produced by Eazy-E (also a member of N.W.A). The album was a huge success for Michel'le, making it to #35 on the Billboard 200 and #5 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart and was certified Gold by the RIAA 5 weeks after its release. Five singles were released "No More Lies", "Nicety", "Keep Watchin'", "Something in My Heart" and "If", The most successful being "No More Lies", which made it to #7 on the Billboard Hot 100 and #2 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.

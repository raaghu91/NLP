"However"   Single by Glay   from the album Review   Released August 6, 1997   Genre Pop rock, power pop, progressive rock   Label Platinum Records   Producer(s) Glay, Masahide Sakuma   Glay singles chronology      Kuchibiru (1997) However (1997) Yuuwaku (1998)      However is the 12th single release by Japanese band Glay on August 6, 1997. It debuted at #1 on the Oricon chart, appearing at that position for 5 weeks in total, selling 1,341,980 copies. The title song was used as ending song for the TV-drama "Ryakudatsu Ai Abunai Onna" (略奪愛・アブない女, lit. "Despoiled Love: Dangerous Woman"?) aired on TBS channel, whose opening theme was another Glay song, "Freeze My Love", and all the music was instrumental versions of Glay's songs.

(November 2008)     Reiterate      Studio album by GRITS   Released September 16, 2008   Recorded 2007-2008   Genre Southern hip hop, Christian hip hop   Length 46:39   Label Revolution Art   GRITS chronology      The Greatest Hits (2007) Reiterate (2008) Quarantine (2010)      Reiterate is the eighth full length album from the Christian hip hop group GRITS. It was released on September 16, 2008, and is the GRITS first release on their newly formed Revolution Art label.    Contents   1 Track listing 2 Awards 3 Tour 4 References    Track listing[edit]  Sky May Fall Walking My Way Fly Away (featuring Mac Powell) Turn It Up Something About You Reminds Me Get It Started Livin Dreams Dusk Till Daw I Run Beautiful Morning (featuring Pigeon John) Say Goodbye (featuring TobyMac & Jade)  Awards[edit] The album was nominated for a Dove Award for Rap/Hip-Hop Album of the Year at the 40th GMA Dove Awards.[1] Tour[edit] In support of the album's release, GRITS went on the road for the '30 Day Tour', which took them nationwide from mid-July through mid-August 2008. They also toured the summer festival circuit including Agape Fest, Ichthus Festival, Crossover Festival, Rock the Desert, Spirit West Coast, and the NewSong Festival. Plans for the 'Art Attack Tour' are also underway for September where GRITS will be joined by other Revolution Art music acts.

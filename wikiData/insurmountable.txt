   Contents   1 English  1.1 Etymology 1.2 Adjective  1.2.1 Translations        English[edit] Etymology[edit] in- +‎ surmountable Adjective[edit] insurmountable (comparative more insurmountable, superlative most insurmountable)  Incapable of being passed over, surmounted, or overcome; insuperable; as, insurmountable difficulty or obstacle.

My formative years were spent in an inner city.   Capable of forming something. (biology) Capable of producing new tissue. (grammar) Pertaining to the inflection of words.  Derived terms[edit]  formativeness  Noun[edit] formative (plural formatives)  (grammar) A language unit that has morphological function.

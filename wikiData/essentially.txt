   Contents   1 English  1.1 Etymology 1.2 Adverb  1.2.1 Synonyms 1.2.2 Translations        English[edit] Etymology[edit] essential +‎ -ly Adverb[edit] essentially (comparative more essentially, superlative most essentially)  In an essential manner; in essence.   2012 March 1, Henry Petroski, “Opening Doors”, American Scientist, volume 100, number 2, page 112-3:   A doorknob of whatever roundish shape is effectively a continuum of levers, with the axis of the latching mechanism—known as the spindle—being the fulcrum about which the turning takes place. Applying a force tangential to the knob is essentially equivalent to applying one perpendicular to a radial line defining the lever.

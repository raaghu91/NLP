With a punctilious slap of the gloves, the duel was now inevitable.   Precise or scrupulous; finicky or nitpicky.  2009, Ronnie Cann, Ruth Kempson and Eleni Gregoromichelaki, Semantics: an introduction to meaning in language  Of course, humans do not treat time in such a punctilious fashion.

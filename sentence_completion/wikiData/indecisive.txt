WOTD – 25 August 2009    Contents   1 English  1.1 Pronunciation 1.2 Adjective  1.2.1 Antonyms 1.2.2 Derived terms 1.2.3 Related terms 1.2.4 Translations        English[edit] Pronunciation[edit]  IPA(key): /ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪv/  Adjective[edit] indecisive (comparative more indecisive, superlative most indecisive)  (of a person) Not decisive; not marked by promptness or decision.  They were all being very indecisive about what to wear to the party.   (of a contest etc.) inconclusive or uncertain  The outcome of the race was so indecisive.

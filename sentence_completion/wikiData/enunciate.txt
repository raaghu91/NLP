Pronunciation[edit]  IPA(key): /ɪˈnʌnsiˌeɪt/ Hyphenation: e‧nun‧ci‧ate  Verb[edit] enunciate (third-person singular simple present enunciates, present participle enunciating, simple past and past participle enunciated)  (transitive) To make a definite or systematic statement of. To announce, proclaim.  Coleridge  the terms in which he enunciates the great doctrines of the gospel     (transitive) To articulate, pronounce.  You must enunciate all the syllables.   (intransitive) To make sounds clearly.  Enunciate when you speak.

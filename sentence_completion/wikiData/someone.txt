It was released in 1987 as the first single from the album If There's Any Justice. The song reached #5 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart.[1] Chart performance[edit]   Chart (1987) Peak position   U.S. Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks 5   Canadian RPM Country Tracks 3   References[edit]   ^ Whitburn, Joel (2004).

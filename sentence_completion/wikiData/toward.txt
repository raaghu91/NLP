(October 2011)       Toward Lighthouse and Foghorn Building   Toward (Scottish Gaelic: Tollard) is a village near Dunoon at the southern tip of the Cowal peninsula in Argyll and Bute, Scotland. Architecture[edit] Nearby is Castle Toward, a former country house built close to the ruined Toward Castle. Now an outdoor education centre, its grounds are used as a location for the children's BBC TV series, Raven.

Adjective[edit] resurgent (comparative more resurgent, superlative most resurgent)  Undergoing a resurgence; experiencing renewed vigor or vitality. (astronomy) Of a celestial object, moving upwards relative to the horizon after a period of having moved downwards. Rising again, as from the dead.

Pronunciation[edit]  (UK, US) IPA(key): /kənˈtɛn.ʃəs/  Adjective[edit] contentious (comparative more contentious, superlative most contentious)  Marked by heated arguments or controversy.   2012 June 19, Phil McNulty, “England 1-0 Ukraine”, BBC Sport:  Ukraine, however, will complain long and hard about a contentious second-half incident when Marko Devic's shot clearly crossed the line before it was scrambled away by John Terry, only for the officials to remain unmoved.      Given to struggling with others out of jealousy or discord.

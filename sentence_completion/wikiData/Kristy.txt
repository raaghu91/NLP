For the village in Slovakia, see Kristy (village). For the list of hurricanes, see Hurricane Kristy (disambiguation).  Kristy is a female name of Latin origin, meaning "follower of Christ". The name can be the shortform, or related to, several names including Christine, Kristina, Kristine, Kristian, or Kristin, the common link being "Kris" meaning "Christ".

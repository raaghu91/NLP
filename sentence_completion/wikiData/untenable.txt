   Contents   1 English  1.1 Etymology 1.2 Adjective  1.2.1 Usage notes 1.2.2 Antonyms 1.2.3 Translations 1.2.4 Related terms   1.3 External links      English[edit] Etymology[edit] un- +‎ tenable Adjective[edit] untenable (comparative more untenable, superlative most untenable)  Not able to be held, as of an opinion or position; unholdable, indefensible.  The theory of cold fusion was untenable.    Usage notes[edit]  Nouns often used with "controversial": argument, doctrine, theory, hypothesis, view, position, notion, etc..

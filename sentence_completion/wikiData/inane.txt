Pronunciation[edit]  IPA(key): /ɪˈneɪn/ Rhymes: -eɪn  Adjective[edit] inane (comparative more inane, superlative most inane)  Lacking sense or meaning (often to the point of boredom or annoyance).  This supremely gifted kid told me that in the early elementary grades, the songs sung in music class were so inane that he wanted to skip grades already! Eventually he did, so better late than never.   purposeless; pointless  I. Taylor  Vague and inane instincts.

Unsourced material may be challenged and removed. (April 2015)       Laughter is a typical expression of joy   Joy is an emotion in response to a pleasant observation or a remembrance thereof. The reason for a joyful reaction is usually that some expectation or need has been satisfied. Joy is usually expressed as a smile, a laughter or exclamation of joy.

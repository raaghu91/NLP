(file)     Adjective[edit] remiss (not comparable)  At fault; failing to fulfill responsibility, duty, or obligations.  I would certainly be remiss if I did not give credit where credit was due.   Not energetic or exact in duty or business; careless; tardy; slack; hence, lacking earnestness or activity; languid; slow.  Milton  Thou never wast remiss, I bear thee witness.   Woodward  Its motion becomes more languid and remiss.

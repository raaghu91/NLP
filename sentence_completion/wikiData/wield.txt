St James's church, Upper Wield, Hampshire   Wield is a civil parish in Hampshire, England, within the district of East Hampshire. It includes two neighbouring villages, Upper Wield and Lower Wield. The parish council meets quarterly at the parish hall in Upper Wield.[1] Wield as a civil parish is separate from the church parish. The parish church, dedicated to St James, is also in Upper Wield.

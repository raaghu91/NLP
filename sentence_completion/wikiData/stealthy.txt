Find sources: "Stealthy" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR · free images (December 2013)          Stealthy  Developer(s) Stealthy   Operating system Cross-platform   Website stealthy.co   Stealthy is a simple browser add-on that lets users visit websites that are restricted based on geographical location, governmental or organizational regulations. It is available as a plugin for Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome. There is also a web proxy service for other browsers and platforms. Current version - 5.0.11 | Released date - January 12, 2015 Stealthy was originally developed to facilitate the dissemination of information during the Arab Spring.[citation needed] It is now used to get past country or organizational limitations, restrictions, and forbidden sites.

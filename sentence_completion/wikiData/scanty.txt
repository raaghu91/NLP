The Scanty (stylized as THE★SCANTY) was a Japanese girls pop-rock band formed in Tokyo on October 2001. They disbanded in October 2003. Futami and Akane formed their own band, Plink?. They performed the two opening themes for the anime series Full Moon o Sagashite, and also made cameo appearances in the show.[1]    Contents   1 Members 2 Discography  2.1 Singles 2.2 Albums 2.3 DVD   3 References 4 Further reading    Members[edit]  Yoppy (Yoshiko Yamamoto)   The vocalist.

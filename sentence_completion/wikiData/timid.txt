(file)     Adjective[edit] timid (comparative timider, superlative timidest)  Lacking in courage or confidence.   1963,  Margery Allingham,  chapter 19, The China Governess[1]:  When Timothy and Julia hurried up the staircase to the bedroom floor, where a considerable commotion was taking place, Tim took Barry Leach with him. […]. The captive made no resistance and came not only quietly but in a series of eager little rushes like a timid dog on a choke chain.      John's a very timid person. I'll doubt he'll be brave enough to face his brother.
